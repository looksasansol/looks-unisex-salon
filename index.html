AEO-ready quick answers

Choose the Right Service Faster

Hair Botox

Usually chosen for a softer, smoother and conditioned-looking finish rather than the strongest straightening effect.

Nanoplastia

Selected when a visibly straighter, glossier result is the main goal—after checking colour and chemical history.

Facial vs D-Tan

A facial covers cleansing and hydration; D-tan specifically targets the visible appearance of surface tanning.

Butterfly vs Step Cut

Butterfly layers focus on face framing and movement; a step cut creates more visible tiered levels.
