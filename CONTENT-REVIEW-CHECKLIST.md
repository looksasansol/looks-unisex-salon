# Content Review Checklist Before Publishing a Draft

- Add at least one real salon experience, client question or stylist insight.
- Replace the AI sample with a real, consented image.
- Confirm every product, process, price and timing statement.
- Remove claims that cannot be proved.
- Keep the answer-first paragraph concise and accurate.
- Add internal links to the service page and 2 relevant articles.
- Check title, description, H1 and image alt text.
- Remove `noindex` only after review.
- Add the URL to sitemap.xml and blog index.
- Inspect the live URL in Google Search Console.
